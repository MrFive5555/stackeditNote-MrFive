$$y'=\frac{4v}{M^2}y(M-y)$$
$$x=\frac{M}{4v}ln(\frac{y}{M-y}/\frac{y_0}{M-y_0})$$
$$y=\frac{y_0}{y_o+(M-y_0)e^{-\frac{4v}{M}x}}$$

---
在初期选取小区间$\Delta x$,且在初期$M-y \approx M$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{y_2}{M-y_2}/\frac{y_1}{M-y_1} & = e^{-\frac{4v}{M}\Delta x} \\
\frac{y_2}{y_1}& \approx 1-\frac{4v}{M}\Delta x
\end{aligned}$$
